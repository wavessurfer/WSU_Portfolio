Software Installation

Visit the Microsoft Azure DevTools website.
Check whether your Office is 32 or 64 bit by opening Excel>Account>About Excel and examining the end of the first line.
If you are still running 32 bit Office please update to the 64 bit Office before proceeding.

Do not mix the 32 bit and 64 bit versions, it will cause you a LOT of problems.
